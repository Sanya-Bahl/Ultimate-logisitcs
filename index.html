ULTIMATE LOGISTICS

________

We specialize in transportation of heavy & over dimensional plants, machinery and equipment. With over 20 years of presence and experience in the industry we have developed expertise in handling and managing Safe & Timely transportation of your high value and sensitive machinery, equipment.


We take pride in all the projects that we handle, whether it is a single transformer weighing over 80MT, a 250 ton crane (SANY SCC2500C) or a complete break-bulk machinery of 500 MT or even the odd size equipments like Boilers, Bullets, Rigs etc. All this with ease, great perfection and in the most timely manner.


Our expertise in project transportation is as diversified as the industries we have served such as Engineering, Oil & Gas, Construction, Refineries, Industrial, Power, Cement etc to name a few. While we take utmost care of your equipments, we also value your “peace of mind” and we deliver both, safely & timely.


We have the most appropriate vehicles [Suspended Axle, Low Bed, Semi Bed etc, the most experienced team and other necessary infrastructure to handle any kind of projects.
